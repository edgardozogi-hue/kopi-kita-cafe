About

CRAFTED IN
THE DARK

ROAST was born from a singular belief — that the perfect cup demands obsession, not approximation. In a dim lit corner of the city, we built a sanctuary where every bean is roasted to its exact moment of peak expression.

We source directly from single origin farms across three continents, forging relationships with growers who share our compulsion for quality. Each batch is small, each pour deliberate, each crema a signature.
